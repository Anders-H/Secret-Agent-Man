=================================
SECRET AGENT MAN - THE VIDEO GAME
=================================

Soon available for the Windows (64-bit) platform, Commodore 64 and Sinclair ZX Spectrum.

---------
STORYLINE
---------

As a secret agent, you are called upon by New York City Mayor Koch to curb the escalating crime rate. Criminal elements are openly roaming among the other citizens, and it is of utmost importance that you eliminate all the criminals in the area, without harming any of the peaceful civilian population.

Programming: Anders Hesselbom
Sound and graphics: Mats J. Larsson

Source code: https://github.com/Anders-H/Secret-Agent-Man
